About the Search Engine and the project

The project was made for the Web Information Retrieval course of Tsinghua University by Catherine Xu. This Search Engine lets you look for wines. You can search for a specific wine from a winery like ' ' or just from a country, varietal type, aroma, taste etc. You can also add a score condition, by showing wines with a score higher than 50,75 or 90. For the price, you can put either a lower and/or an upper limit.
